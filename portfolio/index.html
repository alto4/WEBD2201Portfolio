About Me

Let me tell you a few things about myself and what I do...

I love to create things.

As far back as I can remember, I have always enjoyed building things, and working through projects from start to finish. For years, I have studied as a musician and have worked as a teacher and performer. When I first discovered web development, I immediately felt that same enthusiasm as when I started playing guitar, and knew that building things with code was a career-path that I would like to explore. After taking several courses online on my own volition as a hobby, I decided to draw focus to my learning as a developer and enrolled in the Computer Programming and Analysis advanced diploma program at Durham College in Oshawa, Ontario.
